Hi all! <3

My name is Caelin, I'm a computer science student at the University of Southern Maine, a DJ at my local college radio station WMPG (in a cool transexual way, my artist name is less-than-three), a hobbyist muscician and tech nerd. I've been dicking around with HTMl and CSS since I was a kid, and when a bunch of my friends kept saying "man I wish I knew enough html to make a neocities site" I decided to check it out and make something to show off (and get familiar enough with the site to teach them).

I like making shitty electronic music, programming, and spending time with my wondeful girlfriend. I currently have a radio show on WMPG called New Age Riot Girl, it comes on every Sunday night at 11pm and ends Monday morning at 1am. Links to my archives can be found all over the place (I just can't get enough of self advertisement). The show itself is meant to highlight all kinds of underground music I enjoy from Rock and punk to hiphop to electronic, anything goes as long as it's a little weird and a little niche. Feel free to call in to request a song when I'm on the air at +1 (207) 780-4909.

Besides from my show I plan events at WMPG including our Battle of the Bands. I also work on our Local Motives show. Local Motives features local Maine (mostly Portland) bands, playing live in our studio. I work as an audio engineer on the show and am slowly but surely improving my live audio, and mixing skills.
